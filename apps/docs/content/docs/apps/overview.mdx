Apps extend your Auxx.ai workspace with third-party integrations, automation tools, and specialized features. Browse the marketplace to find apps that connect your existing tools or add new capabilities.

## The marketplace

Go to **Settings > Apps** to browse available apps. Each app shows its name, developer, category, and a brief description.

![Apps marketplace showing installed apps and browsable categories](/images/settings-apps-marketplace.png)

Apps are organized into categories:

| Category | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Analytics** | Data visualization and reporting |
| **Autonomous** | AI and automation tools |
| **Billing** | Payment and accounting integrations |
| **Calling** | Phone and voice integrations |
| **Customer Support** | Support tools and helpdesk connectors |
| **Communication** | Messaging and chat platforms |
| **Forms & Survey** | Form builders and survey tools |
| **Product Management** | Project management integrations |

## App types

There are two types of apps:

- **Production apps** — Published to the marketplace, available to all organizations
- **Development apps** — Private apps targeted to a specific organization, typically used during app development and testing

## Built-in integrations

Some integrations are built directly into Auxx.ai rather than delivered as marketplace apps:

- **Email** (Gmail, Outlook) — Configured in **Settings > Integrations**
- **Shopify** — Configured in **Settings > Shopify** (see [Shopify integration](/apps/shopify))

These built-in integrations have dedicated settings pages with deeper configuration options.
