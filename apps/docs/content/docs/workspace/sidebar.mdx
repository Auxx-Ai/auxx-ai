The sidebar is the main way to navigate Auxx.ai. It's always visible on the left side of the screen and organizes your workspace into collapsible sections.

![Auxx.ai dashboard showing the sidebar](/images/dashboard-inbox.png)

## Sidebar sections

The sidebar is divided into the following groups:

### Me

Your personal mail views:

- **Inbox** — Messages assigned to you, with an unread count
- **Drafts** — Replies you've saved but haven't sent
- **Sent** — Messages you've sent

### Views

Custom saved views that filter your inbox by specific criteria like tags, status, or assignee. Click **Create a view** to set up a new one.

### Shared Inboxes

Team-visible mailboxes that pull in messages from connected integrations (Gmail, Outlook, etc.). Each shared inbox shows its own unread count. Shared inboxes are configured in **Settings > Inboxes**.

### Configurations

Quick access to automation and task management:

- **Workflows** — Automation rules for routing, tagging, and auto-responding
- **Tasks** — Organization-wide task list

### Shopify

If you have a Shopify store connected, this section provides quick access to Shopify-related data. Expand it to see available options.

### Resources and Examples

Expandable sections for additional content and reference material.

### Records

Structured data views for your organization. Default records include:

- **Support Tickets** — Trackable support requests
- **Contacts** — Customer profiles
- **Tickets** — Alternative ticket views

If you've configured custom entities (e.g., Parts, Products), they also appear here.

### Bottom bar

- **Settings** — Opens the full settings panel
- **Notifications** — Alerts and updates
- **Help and resources** — Documentation and support links
- **Trial badge** — Days remaining on your trial (if applicable)

## Customizing the sidebar

### Collapse and expand sections

Click any section header to collapse or expand it. Your collapse state is saved automatically and persists across sessions.

### Toggle the sidebar

Click the **Toggle Sidebar** button at the top of the sidebar to collapse it entirely, giving more space to the main content area. Click again to restore it.

### Reorder and visibility

Use the **Options** menu (hover over any section or item to reveal it) to access additional actions:

- Reorder items within a section
- Show or hide specific inboxes or records
- Customize which sections appear in the sidebar

These preferences are saved per-user, so each team member can personalize their own sidebar layout.

## User menu

Click your name and avatar at the bottom-left of the sidebar to open the user menu.

![User menu showing account and organization options](/images/sidebar-user-menu.png)

From the user menu you can:

| Action | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Upgrade to Pro** | Navigate to plan upgrade |
| **Switch Organization** | Switch between workspaces you belong to |
| **Account** | Go to your account settings |
| **Developer Portal** | Access the developer tools |
| **Billing** | Manage subscription and payment |
| **Theme** | Toggle between light and dark mode |
| **Sign Out** | Log out of your account |
