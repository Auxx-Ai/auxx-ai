title: Members & roles
description: Manage team members, assign roles, and control permissions in your Auxx.ai organization.
---

Every person in your Auxx.ai organization has a role that determines what they can access and configure. Manage members from **Settings > Members**.

![Members page showing team members and their roles](/images/settings-members.png)

## Roles

| Role | Access level |
|------|-------------|
| **Owner** | Full access to everything. Can manage all members (including Admins), delete the organization, and configure all settings. Every organization has at least one owner. |
| **Admin** | Can manage settings, integrations, inboxes, and invite members. Can change User roles but cannot manage other Admins or delete the organization. |
| **User** | Standard access. Can use the inbox, reply to messages, view contacts and tickets, and use workflows — but cannot change organization settings or manage members. |

### What each role can do

| Action | Owner | Admin | User |
|--------|-------|-------|------|
| Read and reply to messages | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| View contacts, tickets, records | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Use workflows | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Connect integrations | Yes | Yes | No |
| Manage shared inboxes | Yes | Yes | No |
| Invite members | Yes | Yes | No |
| Change User roles | Yes | Yes | No |
| Change Admin roles | Yes | No | No |
| Manage organization settings | Yes | Yes | No |
| Delete the organization | Yes | No | No |

## Invite members

1. Go to **Settings > Members**.
2. Click **Invite Member**.
3. Enter the person's email and select a role (Admin or User).
4. Click **Send Invitation**.

![Invite member popover with email and role fields](/images/settings-members-invite.png)

For more details on the invitation flow, see [Invite your team](/getting-started/invite-team).

## Change a member's role

1. Go to **Settings > Members**.
2. Click the three-dot menu next to the member.
3. Select **Change role**.
4. Choose the new role and confirm.

<Callout type="info" title="Role restrictions">
  Admins can only change Users' roles. Only Owners can promote or demote Admins.
</Callout>

## Remove a member

1. Go to **Settings > Members**.
2. Click the three-dot menu next to the member.
3. Select **Remove Member**.
4. Confirm the removal.

The member is removed from the organization immediately and loses access to all workspace data.

## Pending invitations

Invitations that haven't been accepted appear in a separate section on the members page. Each pending invitation shows:

- The invitee's email address
- The assigned role
- When the invitation was sent
- When it expires (7 days after sending)

Use the three-dot menu on a pending invitation to:

- **Resend Invitation** — Send a new email and reset the 7-day expiry
- **Copy Invite Link** — Copy a shareable join link
- **Cancel Invitation** — Revoke the invitation
