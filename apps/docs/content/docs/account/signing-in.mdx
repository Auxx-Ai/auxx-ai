---
title: Signing in
description: Sign in to Auxx.ai with email and password, Google, GitHub, or a passkey for passwordless authentication.
---

Auxx.ai supports multiple ways to sign in. You can use any method linked to your account.

## Sign-in methods

| Method | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Email & password** | Sign in with your email and password. Requires email verification on signup. |
| **Google** | Sign in with your Google account via OAuth. |
| **GitHub** | Sign in with your GitHub account via OAuth. |
| **Passkey** | Passwordless sign-in using biometrics, security keys, or device credentials. |

## Email & password

![Auxx.ai login page with email, Google, and GitHub sign-in options](/images/login-page.png)

1. Go to the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Enter your password
4. Click **Sign In**

If you have [two-factor authentication](/account/2fa) enabled, you'll be prompted for your 6-digit code after entering your password.

### Forgot your password

1. On the login page, click **Forgot password?**
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your inbox for a password reset link
4. Click the link and set a new password

## Google

Click **Continue with Google** on the login page. You'll be redirected to Google to authorize access, then returned to Auxx.ai. If it's your first time, an account is created automatically using your Google profile.

## GitHub

Click **Continue with GitHub** on the login page. You'll be redirected to GitHub to authorize access, then returned to Auxx.ai. If it's your first time, an account is created automatically.

## Passkeys

Passkeys let you sign in without a password using biometrics (Face ID, Touch ID, Windows Hello) or a hardware security key.

### Add a passkey

![Passkeys dialog with name field and Create Passkey button](/images/settings-passkeys-dialog.png)

1. Go to **Settings > General**
2. In the passkeys section, click **Passkeys**
3. Enter a name for the passkey and click **Create Passkey**
4. Follow your browser or device prompts to register the passkey

### Sign in with a passkey

1. On the login page, click **Sign in with Passkey**
2. Follow your browser or device prompts to authenticate

You can register multiple passkeys (e.g., one per device) and remove any from **Settings > General**.

## Linking accounts

If you sign up with Google and later want to also use email/password, you can set a password in **Settings > General**. Your account then supports both methods.
