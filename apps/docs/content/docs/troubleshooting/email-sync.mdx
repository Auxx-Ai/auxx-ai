---
title: Email sync problems
description: Troubleshoot Gmail and Outlook email sync issues in Auxx.ai, including connection failures, missing messages, and sync delays.
---

If your Gmail or Outlook inbox isn't syncing correctly, use the steps below to diagnose and fix the issue.

## Connection issues

<Accordions type="single">
  <Accordion title="My email integration shows as disconnected">
    Go to **Settings > Integrations** and check the status of your connection. If it shows as disconnected or expired:

    1. Click **Reconnect** next to the integration
    2. Complete the OAuth authorization flow again
    3. Verify the connection shows as **Active**

    OAuth tokens can expire if your email provider revokes access or if you changed your email password.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I can't connect my Gmail account">
    - Make sure you're signing in with the correct Google account
    - Check that you've granted all requested permissions during the OAuth flow
    - If your organization uses Google Workspace, your admin may need to allow third-party app access
    - Try removing Auxx.ai from your Google account's connected apps (myaccount.google.com > Security > Third-party apps), then reconnect
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I can't connect my Outlook account">
    - Verify you're signing in with the correct Microsoft account
    - If you're using a work or school account, your IT admin may need to approve the Auxx.ai app
    - Check that you've granted all requested permissions
    - Try clearing your browser cookies for microsoft.com and re-authorizing
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>

## Missing messages

<Accordions type="single">
  <Accordion title="New emails aren't appearing in Auxx.ai">
    - Check that the integration is active in **Settings > Integrations**
    - New messages are delivered via webhooks — there may be a short delay
    - Verify the email was sent to the address connected to Auxx.ai
    - Check your inbox filters — the message may be in a different view
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Sent replies aren't showing in my email client">
    Replies sent from Auxx.ai are sent via your connected email provider. If they're not appearing in your sent folder:

    - Check the **Sent** folder in Auxx.ai to confirm the message was sent successfully
    - Some email configurations may not automatically save sent messages — check your provider's settings
    - If the reply shows as failed in Auxx.ai, check the error message for details
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Old emails aren't syncing">
    Auxx.ai syncs messages going forward from the time you connect your inbox. Historical emails are not imported retroactively. Only new incoming and outgoing messages after connection are tracked.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>

## Sync delays

<Accordions type="single">
  <Accordion title="There's a delay between receiving an email and seeing it in Auxx.ai">
    A short delay (usually under a minute) is normal. Email sync relies on webhooks from Gmail or Outlook. If delays are consistently longer:

    - Check if the integration is still active
    - Disconnect and reconnect the integration to re-register webhooks
    - Check Auxx.ai's status page for any ongoing service issues
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Emails are arriving out of order">
    Messages are displayed by their received timestamp. If emails from different senders arrive at nearly the same time, they may appear in a different order than expected. This is normal behavior.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>

## Shared inboxes

<Accordions type="single">
  <Accordion title="Messages aren't routing to the correct shared inbox">
    Shared inboxes are tied to specific email addresses. Make sure:

    - The incoming email is addressed to the correct shared inbox email
    - The inbox is configured correctly in **Settings > Inboxes**
    - The integration for that email address is active
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Team members can't see shared inbox messages">
    Check that the team members are added to the shared inbox. Go to **Settings > Inboxes**, select the inbox, and verify the member list.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
