# Plans overview

Auxx.ai offers four plans designed to scale with your team. Every new workspace starts on the **Free** plan, and you can upgrade at any time.

## Available plans

Auxx.ai offers four plans: **Free**, **Starter**, **Growth**, and **Enterprise**. For full pricing and feature comparison, visit [auxx.ai/pricing](https://auxx.ai/pricing).

## Feature limits

Each plan includes monthly usage quotas for emails and AI requests. When you reach a limit, that feature is paused until the next billing cycle. You can upgrade your plan at any time to increase your limits.

Limits are tracked per workspace — not per user. You can view your current usage in **Settings > Plans**.

## Trials

Starter and Growth plans include a **14-day free trial**. During the trial you get full access to all plan features. No credit card is required to start a trial.

Each workspace is eligible for one trial. If you cancel during the trial period, you won't be charged.

## Viewing your current plan

Go to **Settings > Plans** to see your active plan, billing cycle, usage, and available upgrades.

![Plans & Billing settings page showing current plan, billing details, and invoice history](/images/settings-plans.png)

To compare plans and switch, click **Change Plan** and then **Change plan** inside the dialog to open the plan picker.

![Plan picker showing Starter, Growth, Enterprise, and Free plans with pricing and features](/images/settings-plans-picker.png)
