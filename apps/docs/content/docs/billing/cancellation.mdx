You can cancel your Auxx.ai subscription at any time. Your workspace remains active until the end of your current billing period.

## Cancel your subscription

![Cancel Your Subscription dialog with reason selector, feedback field, and confirmation input](/images/settings-plans-cancel.png)

1. Go to **Settings > Plans**
2. Click **Cancel Subscription**
3. Select a reason for canceling:
   - Too expensive
   - Missing features I need
   - Switching to another service
   - Just needed it temporarily
   - Other reason
4. Optionally add feedback
5. Type **Cancel subscription** to confirm
6. Click **Confirm Cancellation**

## What happens after cancellation

- **Immediate**: Your subscription is marked for cancellation, but you keep full access to all paid features until the end of your billing period.
- **End of billing period**: Your workspace is downgraded to the Free plan. Feature limits from the Free plan are enforced — excess team members are deactivated and usage quotas are reduced.
- **Data retention**: Your data (tickets, contacts, conversations, integrations) is retained. If your workspace remains inactive beyond the retention period, it may be scheduled for deletion. You'll receive notification emails before any data is removed.

## Reactivate your subscription

Changed your mind? You can restore your subscription before the billing period ends:

1. Go to **Settings > Plans**
2. Click **Restore Subscription**

This cancels the scheduled downgrade and your subscription continues as before with no interruption.

### Reactivate after expiration

If your billing period has already ended and your workspace was downgraded, you can reactivate by upgrading to a paid plan:

1. Go to **Settings > Plans**
2. Select a plan and complete checkout

Your existing data and settings are preserved — you'll pick up right where you left off.
