Your account settings let you update your profile information, change your email or password, and manage your active sessions.

## Profile

Go to **Settings > General** to view and edit your profile.

![General settings page showing profile photo, name, email, timezone, and account provider](/images/settings-account.png)

You can update:

- **Avatar** — Click your profile photo to upload a new image
- **Name** — Your display name shown throughout the app
- **Email** — Your login email (changing it requires verification)

### Change your email

1. Go to **Settings > General**
2. Click **Edit** next to your email address
3. Enter the new email address
4. A verification link is sent to the new address
5. Click the link to confirm — your email is updated once verified

Your current email remains active until the new one is verified. The verification link expires after 24 hours.

### Change your password

1. Go to **Settings > General**
2. In the password section, click **Change Password**
3. Enter your current password and a new password
4. Click **Save**

Passwords must be at least 8 characters.

<Callout type="info" title="Social login accounts">
  If you signed up with Google or GitHub and don't have a password set, you can add one from the password section. This lets you also sign in with email and password.
</Callout>

## Sessions

The settings page shows your current session information, including device type, operating system, and browser. You can sign out from your current session here.
