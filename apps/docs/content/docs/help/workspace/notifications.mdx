---

Auxx.ai sends in-app notifications to keep you informed about important events — new assignments, mentions, status changes, and more.

## Notification center

Click the **bell icon** in the bottom-left of the sidebar to open the notification center. From here you can:

- View your unread notifications
- Mark individual notifications as read
- Mark all notifications as read
- Delete notifications you no longer need

## Notification types

Auxx.ai sends notifications for events like:

| Event | When you're notified |
|-------|---------------------|
| **Assignment** | A conversation or ticket is assigned to you |
| **Mention** | Someone mentions you in a message or comment |
| **Status change** | A ticket or conversation you're involved in changes status |
| **Comment reply** | Someone replies to a comment thread you're part of |
| **Workflow completion** | An automation workflow you set up finishes running |

## Managing notifications

Notifications are delivered in real time within the app. The bell icon shows a count of unread notifications so you can stay on top of activity without constantly checking.

To keep your notification list manageable:

- **Mark as read** — Click on a notification to mark it as read, or use the bulk action to clear all
- **Delete** — Remove notifications you no longer need
- **Click to navigate** — Clicking a notification takes you directly to the relevant conversation, ticket, or record
