---
title: Organizations
description: Create, switch between, and manage organizations in Auxx.ai.
---

An organization is the container for everything in your Auxx.ai workspace — inboxes, integrations, team members, tickets, and settings. You can belong to multiple organizations and switch between them at any time.

## Your organizations

Go to **Settings > Organization** to see all organizations you belong to.

![Organization settings page showing memberships](/images/settings-organization.png)

Each organization shows:

- **Name** — The organization's display name
- **Default** badge — Indicates which organization loads when you sign in
- **Role** — Your role in the organization (Owner, Admin, or User)
- **Type** — Team Organization or Individual

## Create an organization

1. Click your name at the bottom-left of the sidebar to open the user menu.
2. Select **Switch Organization**.
3. Click **Add Organization**.
4. Enter a name and handle for the new organization.
5. Click **Create**.

The new organization is created with you as the owner. You can then invite team members, connect integrations, and configure settings.

## Switch organizations

You can switch between organizations in two ways:

- **User menu** — Click your name in the sidebar, select **Switch Organization**, and choose the workspace you want.
- **Settings** — Go to **Settings > Organization** and click on the organization to switch to.

When you switch, all data in the interface updates to reflect the selected organization — inbox, contacts, settings, and integrations all change to the new workspace context.

## Set a default organization

Your default organization is the one that loads automatically when you sign in. To change it, switch to the organization you want as your default — Auxx.ai remembers your last active organization.

## Leave an organization

1. Go to **Settings > Organization**.
2. Click **Leave** next to the organization.

<Callout type="warn" title="Owner restriction">
  You cannot leave an organization if you are the sole owner. Transfer ownership to another member first, or delete the organization instead.
</Callout>

## Delete an organization

Only owners can delete an organization.

1. Go to **Settings > Organization**.
2. Click **Delete** next to the organization.
3. Confirm the deletion.

<Callout type="error" title="This action is permanent">
  Deleting an organization removes all its data — inboxes, integrations, members, tickets, contacts, and settings. This cannot be undone.
</Callout>
