---
title: Payment methods
description: Add, update, or remove payment methods for your Auxx.ai subscription, and manage your billing address.
---

Auxx.ai uses [Stripe](https://stripe.com) to process payments. You can manage your payment methods and billing details from **Settings > Plans**.

![Billing Details section showing Address and Payment cards](/images/settings-plans.png)

## Add a payment method

1. Go to **Settings > Plans**
2. Scroll to the **Payment Methods** section
3. Click **Add Payment Method**
4. Enter your card details in the Stripe form
5. Click **Save**

The new card is added to your account. If it's your first payment method, it's automatically set as the default.

## Set a default payment method

Your default payment method is used for all subscription charges and renewals.

1. Go to **Settings > Plans**
2. In the **Payment Methods** section, find the card you want to use
3. Click the menu on the card and select **Set as default**

## Remove a payment method

1. Go to **Settings > Plans**
2. In the **Payment Methods** section, find the card you want to remove
3. Click the menu on the card and select **Delete**
4. Confirm the removal

You cannot remove your only payment method while you have an active paid subscription.

## Update billing address

Your billing address appears on invoices and is used for tax calculations.

1. Go to **Settings > Plans**
2. Scroll to the **Billing Details** section
3. Update your company name, email, or address fields
4. Click **Save**

The billing address supports full international addresses including street, city, state/province, postal code, and country.

## Billing portal

For advanced billing management, you can access the Stripe billing portal directly. Click **Manage Billing** in the Plans settings page to open the portal, where you can:

- View and update all payment methods
- Download invoices
- Update billing information
