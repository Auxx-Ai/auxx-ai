---
title: Upgrading & downgrading
description: Learn how to upgrade, downgrade, or change billing cycles for your Auxx.ai subscription.
---

You can change your plan at any time from **Settings > Plans**. Upgrades take effect immediately, and downgrades are applied at the end of your current billing period.

## Upgrade your plan

1. Go to **Settings > Plans**
2. Select the plan you want to upgrade to
3. Choose your billing cycle — **Monthly** or **Annual** (30% savings)
4. Complete checkout through Stripe

After checkout, your new plan features are available immediately. If you're upgrading mid-cycle, you'll receive a prorated credit for the unused portion of your current plan.

![Plan picker showing available plans with pricing and feature comparison](/images/settings-plans-picker.png)

After selecting a plan, you'll see the Change Summary with billing cycle, seats, billing address, payment details, and a cost breakdown.

![Change Summary dialog with billing period, seats, billing address, payment form, and price breakdown](/images/settings-plans-change.png)

## Start a free trial

If your workspace hasn't used a trial before, you can start a **14-day free trial** of the Starter or Growth plan:

1. Go to **Settings > Plans**
2. Click **Start 14-Day Free Trial** on the plan you want to try
3. No credit card required — you'll get full access to all plan features

At the end of the trial, you can choose to subscribe or return to the Free plan.

## Downgrade your plan

1. Go to **Settings > Plans**
2. Select a lower-tier plan
3. Confirm the change

Downgrades are **scheduled for the end of your current billing period**. You keep full access to your current plan until then.

### What happens when you downgrade

When the downgrade takes effect, Auxx.ai enforces the new plan's limits:

- **Team members** — If you exceed the new plan's member limit, the most recently added members are deactivated first (regular users before admins). The workspace owner is never deactivated.
- **Connected inboxes** — Excess integrations may be disabled.
- **Usage quotas** — Monthly email and AI request limits are reduced to match the new plan.

## Change your billing cycle

You can switch between monthly and annual billing:

1. Go to **Settings > Plans**
2. Toggle the billing cycle from **Monthly** to **Annual** (or vice versa)
3. Confirm the change

Switching to annual saves 30%. The change is prorated — you'll pay the difference or receive a credit for the remaining time.

## Scheduled changes

When you schedule a plan change (downgrade or billing cycle switch), you'll see the upcoming change displayed on your plan settings page. You can cancel a scheduled change at any time before it takes effect.
