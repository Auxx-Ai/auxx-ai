title: Invoices & receipts

Auxx.ai generates an invoice for every payment. You can view your full billing history and download PDF receipts from **Settings > Plans**.

![Invoice History section on the Plans & Billing page](/images/settings-plans.png)

## View invoices

1. Go to **Settings > Plans**
2. Scroll to the **Invoices** section

Invoices are listed newest first with the following details:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Invoice #** | Unique invoice reference number |
| **Date** | When the invoice was generated |
| **Amount** | Total charged (in your billing currency) |
| **Status** | Current payment status |
| **Download** | Link to download the PDF |

The list loads more invoices as you scroll.

## Invoice statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Paid** | Payment was successfully processed |
| **Upcoming** | Invoice is generated but not yet due |
| **Pending** | Payment is due but hasn't been collected yet |
| **Failed** | Payment attempt was unsuccessful |
| **Void** | Invoice was canceled and no payment is expected |

If an invoice shows **Failed**, check that your default payment method is valid. Go to **Payment Methods** to update your card, and the next automatic retry should succeed.

## Download a PDF

Click the download button next to any invoice to save a PDF receipt. The PDF includes your billing address, line items, taxes, and payment details.
