To get started with Auxx.ai, you need to create an account. You can sign up using an existing Google or GitHub account, or with your email address or phone number.

![Sign-up page with email, phone, Google, and GitHub options](/images/signup-page.png)

## Sign-up methods

Auxx.ai supports four ways to create an account:

| Method | How it works |
|--------|-------------|
| **Google** | One-click sign-up using your Google account. No password needed. |
| **GitHub** | One-click sign-up using your GitHub account. No password needed. |
| **Email** | Enter your email and create a password. You'll need to verify your email before signing in. |
| **Phone** | Enter your phone number and verify with a 6-digit code sent via SMS. |

## Sign up with Google or GitHub

1. Go to [auxx.ai](https://auxx.ai) and click **Sign up**.
2. Click **Login with Google** or **Login with Github**.
3. Authorize Auxx.ai to access your account.
4. You'll be redirected to the onboarding flow to set up your profile and organization.

<Callout type="info" title="Linking multiple methods">
  You can link additional sign-in methods later from your account settings. For example, sign up with Google and add a password afterward.
</Callout>

## Sign up with email

1. Go to [auxx.ai](https://auxx.ai) and click **Sign up**.
2. Click **Sign up with Email**.
3. Enter your email address and create a password.

![Email sign-up form with password requirements](/images/signup-email-form.png)

Your password must contain:
- At least 8 characters
- At least 1 number
- At least 1 lowercase letter
- At least 1 uppercase letter

4. Click **Create Account**.
5. Check your inbox for a verification email and click the link to confirm your address.
6. After verification, sign in and you'll be taken to the onboarding flow.

<Callout type="warn" title="Check your spam folder">
  If you don't see the verification email within a few minutes, check your spam or junk folder.
</Callout>

## Sign up with phone

1. Go to [auxx.ai](https://auxx.ai) and click **Sign up**.
2. Click **Sign up with Phone**.
3. Select your country and enter your phone number.
4. Click **Send Code**.
5. Enter the 6-digit verification code sent to your phone.
6. Once verified, you'll be taken to the onboarding flow.

## What happens after sign-up

After creating your account, Auxx.ai automatically:

1. **Creates your organization** — A default workspace is set up for you with you as the owner.
2. **Starts the onboarding flow** — A 4-step guided setup walks you through configuring your account.

### Onboarding steps

<Steps>
<Step>

### Personal info

Enter your first name, last name, and optionally upload a profile photo.

</Step>
<Step>

### Organization details

Name your organization and choose a unique handle (e.g., `acme-corp`). You can optionally add your website URL.

</Step>
<Step>

### Connect an inbox

Connect your Gmail or Outlook account to start receiving messages. You can skip this step and do it later.

</Step>
<Step>

### Invite your team

Invite team members by email and assign them a role (Member or Admin). You can also copy an invite link to share. This step is optional.

</Step>
</Steps>

After completing onboarding, you'll land on your dashboard and can start using Auxx.ai.

## Joining via invitation

If someone on your team has already set up an organization, they can invite you by email. When you sign up using the invited email address:

- You'll automatically join their organization instead of creating a new one.
- The personal info onboarding step is skipped.
- You'll be taken directly to the dashboard as a member of the existing organization.
