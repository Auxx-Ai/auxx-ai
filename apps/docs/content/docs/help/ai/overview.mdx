Auxx.ai uses AI to help your support team respond faster and more consistently. AI features are woven throughout the platform — from composing replies to automating entire workflows.

## What AI can do

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Compose replies** | Generate draft responses based on conversation context and your knowledge base |
| **Rewrite text** | Rephrase selected text to be clearer, shorter, or more professional |
| **Adjust tone** | Change the tone of a message (friendly, formal, empathetic, etc.) |
| **Translate** | Translate messages to other languages |
| **Fix grammar** | Correct spelling and grammar issues |
| **Expand or shorten** | Make text longer with more detail, or condense it |
| **Workflow AI nodes** | Use AI within automation workflows to generate content, make decisions, and process data |

## How AI generates replies

When you use AI to compose or suggest a reply, Auxx.ai gathers context from multiple sources:

1. **Conversation history** — The last several messages in the thread, so AI understands the full context
2. **Knowledge base** — Articles and documents you've added to your datasets
3. **Shopify data** — Customer profiles, order details, and product information from connected stores
4. **Custom fields** — Any structured data attached to the contact or ticket

The AI combines this context with your request and generates a response using your configured AI model. You always review and edit the draft before sending.

## AI in the inbox

When viewing a conversation, AI tools are available in the reply composer:

- **AI compose** — Generate a full reply based on the conversation
- **AI rewrite** — Select text and choose an operation (rewrite, tone, translate, grammar, expand, shorten)

All AI-generated content appears as a draft — you have full control to edit, approve, or discard before sending.

## AI in workflows

Workflows can include AI nodes that automatically process incoming messages:

- **Generate responses** — Draft replies using AI and conversation context
- **Classify messages** — Categorize incoming messages by topic or intent
- **Extract data** — Pull structured information from unstructured text
- **Make decisions** — Route conversations based on AI analysis

Combined with [approval nodes](/help/ai/workflow-approvals), you can build human-in-the-loop automations where AI drafts a reply and a team member approves it before sending.

## Usage and quotas

AI usage is tracked per organization. Your plan determines how many AI tokens you can use per month:

- **Free tier** — Limited system credits
- **Paid tiers** — Monthly credit allowance based on plan level

You can view your usage in **Settings > AI Models**. If you bring your own API keys, usage counts against your provider account instead of Auxx.ai credits.
