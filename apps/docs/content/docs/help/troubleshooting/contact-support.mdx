If you can't resolve an issue using the documentation, the Auxx.ai support team is here to help.

## How to reach us

| Channel | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Email** | support@auxx.ai |
| **In-app** | Click **Help and resources** in the sidebar, then select **Contact Support** |

## What to include

To help us resolve your issue quickly, include the following in your message:

- **Your organization name** — so we can locate your account
- **Description of the issue** — what happened, what you expected, and what you see instead
- **Steps to reproduce** — the exact steps that trigger the problem
- **Screenshots or screen recordings** — visual context helps us understand the issue faster
- **Browser and OS** — e.g., Chrome on macOS, Firefox on Windows
- **Error messages** — copy the full text of any errors you see

## Common information for specific issues

### Integration problems
- Which integration (Gmail, Outlook, Shopify)
- The email address or store domain connected
- When the issue started

### Sync issues
- What data type is affected (products, orders, customers, emails)
- Whether the issue is with manual sync, automatic sync, or both
- Any error messages from the sync job history

### Billing issues
- Your current plan name
- The specific billing action you're trying to take
- Any error messages from the payment process

## Response times

Our support team aims to respond within one business day. Complex issues may require additional time to investigate.
