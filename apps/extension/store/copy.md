```
Auxx.ai is the AI-powered CRM and helpdesk for modern teams. This extension lets
you save contacts and companies straight into your Auxx.ai workspace from the
sites where you actually meet them — LinkedIn, Gmail, X, Facebook, Instagram,
and any company website.

Click the Auxx.ai button on a profile or thread, and the extension parses the
relevant fields (name, email, phone, company, domain) and creates the record
in your Auxx.ai workspace with one click. If a matching contact already exists,
Auxx.ai surfaces it instead of creating a duplicate.

Supported sites:
• LinkedIn — person profiles, company profiles, Sales Navigator, search results
• Gmail — pull recipients out of a thread or compose
• X / Twitter — profile pages, search lists
• Facebook — public profiles (via the About → Contact and Basic Info tab)
• Instagram — public profiles
• Any company website — capture the page as a company record

Requires an Auxx.ai account at https://auxx.ai. New here? Start a free trial.

Privacy: the extension only reads the page DOM on the sites listed above, only
when you activate it. It sends the parsed contact data to your Auxx.ai workspace
and nothing else. Full policy at https://auxx.ai/privacy-policy.
```
